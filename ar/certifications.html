التوافق مع السوق الإماراتي

Our European certifications work alongside UAE federal و emirate requirements. MT is currently completing registration with the Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme (ECAS) under the Ministry of Industry و Advanced Technology (MoIAT) — the federal mandatory framework for water fittings.

Because ESMA accepts test reports from ISO/IEC 17025-accredited laboratories (the same labs behind WRAS, KIWA و ACS), our European documentation accelerates the ECAS dossier substantially. Complementary approvals from Dubai Municipality و DEWA are pursued on a project-by-project basis.
