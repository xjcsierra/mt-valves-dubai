Check Valves

Non-return valves to prevent backflow in pressurised piping systems. Available in brass for sanitary networks and cast iron with double disc for industrial pipework.

Reliable backflow prevention from residential to industrial

MT check valves prevent reverse flow without operator intervention — the disc/ball lifts under forward flow and seats automatically against backpressure. Three families: Light Type (brass body, metallic shutter, ½″–2″ for sanitary networks), Heavy Type (reinforced brass for industrial duty up to PN-25, threaded F-F BSP), and Bottom valve with strainer foot (suction lines, immersed pump intakes) up to 2½″.

Stainless steel range (AISI 316/CF8M) for chemical, food and pharmaceutical lines. Cast iron range (GG-25 / GJL-250) with epoxy coating for HVAC and water treatment. Working pressures up to PN-16 for cast iron and PN-25 for brass. Operating temperature -10 °C to +120 °C typical, depending on series.
