Ball Valves

Forged brass and stainless steel ball valves built for water, gas, heating and industrial applications. Full-bore design, BSP threaded connections, ISO 228 compliant.

126
References in Dubai stock

Engineered for water, gas and demanding industrial service

MT ball valves are designed for reliable shut-off in pressurised water, gas, heating, oil and chemical applications. The full-bore design minimises pressure drop, while the floating-ball arrangement ensures self-energised sealing — system pressure forces the ball against the downstream seat, providing bubble-tight closure without external adjustment.

Available in two-piece and three-piece body configurations from DN8 to DN150, with maximum operating pressures up to PN-30 for the threaded Carla series and PN-25 for the standard PN-25 series. Bodies forged in CW617N brass for hydraulic service, or AISI 316 / 1.4408 stainless steel for chemical, food and pharmaceutical applications.

All threaded ends comply with ISO 228 (parallel) or ISO 7-1 / EN 10226-1 (tapered). Seats in PTFE + 15% glass fibre as standard, with FKM/Viton stem packing rated for -15°C to +90°C. Anti-static device and blow-out proof stem on stainless steel models per ATEX requirements.
