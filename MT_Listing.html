Brass Ball Valves · PN-30

Forged brass ball valves rated for 30 bar working pressure, designed for high-pressure plumbing networks, hotel MEP installations and industrial water systems across the GCC. The PN-30 family covers 8 nominal sizes from 1/4" to 2", with both F-F (female-female) and M-F (male-female) threaded ends per ISO 228-1 BSP standard. Handles are colour-coded — red for general purpose, blue for cold water and DVGW potable water, black for industrial applications — with a choice of carbon steel ergonomic levers or stainless steel butterfly handles for premium installations. All references in this collection are certified for potable water use, CE-marked, and manufactured in Spain at MT's Pallejà facility (Barcelona) under ISO 9001:2015 quality management. Stock is held locally at our Dubai Industrial City hub for 48-hour delivery to UAE addresses.

About Brass Ball Valves PN-30

MT's PN-30 brass ball valve range is designed for high-pressure plumbing and process water applications. The forged brass body (CW617N, dezincification-resistant) provides excellent corrosion performance in chlorinated water, while the chrome-plated brass ball with PTFE seats ensures bubble-tight closure even at full PN-30 (30 bar) working pressure.

All references in this collection are certified for potable water use (ACS, WRAS-pending), CE-marked, and manufactured in Spain at MT's Pallejà facility (Barcelona) under ISO 9001:2015 quality management. Stock is held locally at our Dubai Industrial City hub for 48-hour delivery to UAE addresses and 72-hour delivery across the GCC.

Typical Applications

  • Hotel & hospitality MEP installations
  • Residential developments — risers, isolation valves
  • Commercial buildings — chiller plant rooms, irrigation
  • Industrial water systems — process, cooling, RO/UF
  • Marine & coastal projects — DZR brass body

UAE Compliance

Suitable for projects under Dubai Municipality (DM) plumbing regulations, ESMA-registered for UAE entry, and accepted on Saudi SASO-compliant projects.
