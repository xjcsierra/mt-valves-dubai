UAE Market Compliance

Our European certifications work alongside UAE federal and emirate requirements. MT is currently completing registration with the Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme (ECAS) under the Ministry of Industry and Advanced Technology (MoIAT) — the federal mandatory framework for water fittings.

Because ESMA accepts test reports from ISO/IEC 17025-accredited laboratories (the same labs behind WRAS, KIWA and ACS), our European documentation accelerates the ECAS dossier substantially. Complementary approvals from Dubai Municipality and DEWA are pursued on a project-by-project basis.
