Other GCC countries

For Oman, Qatar, Bahrain and Kuwait, the recommended channel is direct B2B. We coordinate logistics with our customs agents to provide door-to-door delivery in 48-72 hours.

Returns and warranty

All MT products carry a 24-month manufacturer warranty against material and manufacturing defects. Returns through Amazon.ae and Noon are managed under each platform's standard policy. For B2B orders, returns are managed directly with our team within 14 days of receipt.
