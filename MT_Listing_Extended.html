Butterfly Valves · Gold Series (Family 5114)

Universal wafer/lug butterfly valves with ductile cast iron body GGG40 + epoxy 150µm coating, stainless steel disc (1.4408) and EPDM/NBR/FKM seat ring. Working pressures PN-10/16, sizes DN25 to DN600. Available with hand lever, gearbox, electric or pneumatic actuators. The 5114 family covers 57 standard references in our PIM, plus made-to-order configurations for special sizes, actuators and seat compounds.
